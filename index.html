Running Start Program

The RVC Running Start Program allows for students to advance into their careers following high school graduation! Students can obtain an Associate degree meanwhile still earning their high school diploma, and then they can transfer to a four-year institution with the equipped social skills to be successful. When a student is involved in the Running Start program, they get free tuition! No debt after college, and you graduate sooner! This is every student's dream opportunity. Trio Services

Rock Valley College is proud to have our Trio Student Support Services. They service over 140 low-income students, and students with disabilities. Trio provides instructional support and help students overcome barriers associated with class, academic, social, and cultural challenges; in order to complete and advance in their educational pursuits. We assign life coaches, mentorship, support, goal setting, study skills, and much more. We welcome new registered students, and encourage you to reach out to our department by filling out our online application.
